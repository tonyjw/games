🐕 Pup Paradise Path 🐕

A Circular Dog-Themed Board Game Adventure!


How to Play

🎯 Goal

Be the first to collect one Tennis Ball + one Bone + one Stuffie to trigger the endgame. Then the player with the most total toys wins!

🎲 Gameplay

  • Take turns spinning the spinner (1-6)
  • Move clockwise around the 15-tile circular board
  • Land on different tile types for various effects
  • Collect coins and buy toys from Dog Shops

🟢 Tile Types

  • Good (5): Earn 1 coin + spin again
  • Super Good (2): Earn 2 coins + spin again
  • Bad (5): Go back to turn start position
  • Dog Shop (3): Buy toys with coins

🛍️ Shop Items

  • 🎾 Tennis Ball: 1 coin
  • 🦴 Bone: 2 coins
  • 🧸 Stuffie: 3 coins

🐕 Dog Abilities

Each dog character has a special ability to help them on their journey!
